About

Catfight is a 2-player projectile combat-platformer. Think Super Smash Brothers with items only. Fight on four unique levels and collect 10 devastating weapons.

Instructions

Download the zip-file below and run the CatFight.exe executable file. You can play this game with gaming pads, XBox 360 gam pads are recommended.

Game Manual

Red Jelly: this projectile deals a modest amount of damage and knockback. Green Jelly: this projectile deals a minimal amount of damage but significant knockback.
Banana: this projectile deals no damage. Upon impact, it creates a pealed banana which causes players to slip. Umbrella this projectile deals no damamge. When immobile, it summons a lightning bolt from the sky, dealing significant damage.
Morty: this projectile deals no damamge. When immobile, it summons a "ball carrier" which pursues the nearest player. Traffic Cone: this projectile deals no damamge. When immobile, it summons a bus which deals significant damage and knockback.
Mine: this projectile deals no damage. Upon impact, it creates an armed mine which explodes when players collide with it. Tide Pod: this projectile deals no damamge. When immobile, it summons a washing machine which attracts all players.
Green Jelly: this projectile deals a minimal amount of damage and knockback. Upon impact, it sets platforms on fire. Teddy Bear: this projectile deals no damamge. Explodes after a few seconds dealing significatn damage and knockback.
