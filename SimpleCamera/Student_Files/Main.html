Please use a browser that supports HTML-5 "canvas"

Press w/a/s/d to move around the scene

Press r/f/q/e to pan the camera. Try shooting up into the sky!

Press o/p arrow keys to raise and lower the arms of Robit.

Move around the scene to help Robit find his donut! If you find it, make sure to click and drag to rotate it!

Animations for Robit:

Press (space) to pause all the animations.

Press t to see only the transforms!
